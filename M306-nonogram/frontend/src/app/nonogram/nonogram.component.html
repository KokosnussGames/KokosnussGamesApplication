How to play

  • Use the numbers on the left and top as clues.
  • Each number shows how many connected cells must be filled.
  • Click a cell to switch between filled and X.
  • Press Shift and click a cell to empty the cell
  • Drag with the left mouse button to fill multiple cells.
  • Drag with the right mouse button to mark multiple cells with X.
  • Hold Shift while dragging to clear cells.
  • Completed rows and columns are grey. Impossible rows and columns are red.
